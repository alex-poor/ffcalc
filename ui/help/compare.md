# Comparison

Two modes via the toggle:

- **Scenarios** — up to 4 saved scenarios side-by-side. Best offer per row is highlighted.
- **vs Current PHO** — one practice, your offer against their current PHO offer, exportable as PDF.

## Using this screen

- **Add scenario** — pick from any practice's saved scenarios. Switch to vs-Current mode if you want a single-practice comparison instead.
- Click a scenario name to open it in the Workbench.
- Streams that are $0 across every column are hidden to keep the table tight.

## Connections

- Click a scenario → **Workbench** for that practice with that scenario loaded.
- Scenarios come from the **Workbench** (Save as scenario).
